Privacy Policy

Vibe AI ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our service.

1. Data We Collect

  • OAuth tokens obtained via Google and Spotify OAuth2 for the sole purpose of creating and managing playlists on your behalf.
  • User-uploaded images for playlist generation
  • Playlist metadata including selected tracks, titles, and image captions
  • We do not collect or store any personally identifying information such as your name, email address, or Google profile details.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To analyze uploaded images and generate playlists based on your preferences
  • To create and save music playlists on your Spotify or YouTube account
  • To store recent interactions in order to improve user experience

3. Data Protection & Security

We take the protection of your data seriously and follow industry best practices to safeguard it:

  • All communication between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • OAuth tokens are stored securely in an access-controlled environment that is not publicly accessible.
  • Access to stored data is restricted to authorized processes and personnel, following the principle of least privilege.
  • Stored credentials are used only to perform the actions you explicitly request and are never shared with third parties.
  • We regularly review and update our security procedures to protect against unauthorized access.

4. Data Retention

Uploaded images, playlist metadata, and OAuth tokens are retained only as long as necessary to provide the service you request.

You may request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting chamgeon5487@gmail.com. Upon verification, your data will be removed promptly.

5. Your Rights

  • You may revoke OAuth2 access via your Google or Spotify account settings at any time.
  • You may request access to or deletion of your data by emailing chamgeon5487@gmail.com.
